Our Journey

A story of passion, tradition, and the love of authentic Indian cuisine

From Humble Beginnings

In a small village in Gujarat, young Mohan Suryavanshi would wake up before sunrise to help his grandmother in the kitchen. She taught him that cooking wasn't just about following recipes‚Äîit was about understanding the soul of each ingredient, respecting tradition, and cooking with love.

Those early morning lessons, filled with the aroma of freshly ground spices and the warmth of the clay oven, planted a seed that would grow into something beautiful. Mohan's grandmother always said, "Food is the language of love, beta. When you cook for someone, you're sharing a piece of your heart."

After years of training under master chefs in Delhi and Jaipur, Mohan returned to Mumbai with a dream: to create a place where families could experience the authentic flavors of home-cooked Indian meals, prepared with the same care his grandmother had taught him.

Our Values & Commitment

Authenticity First: We never compromise on traditional recipes or cooking methods. Every dish is prepared the way it has been for generations.

Quality Ingredients: Our spices come from Kerala and Rajasthan. Our paneer is made fresh daily. We source vegetables from local organic farms.

Family Values: We treat every guest like family. Many of our staff have been with us for over 8 years, and they know our regular customers by name.

Community Support: We support local farmers and suppliers, contribute to community events, and provide meals to those in need during festivals.
